## Design Context

### Users
Warehouse staff at a grocery distribution company — admins, managers, site managers, and floor employees. Used daily in a warehouse environment (dim lighting, desktop/laptop, focused operational context). Users need speed and clarity above all else.

### Brand Personality
Precise · Operational · Trustworthy — a serious tool for serious work. Not flashy, not playful. It should feel like a high-end professional instrument.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark neumorphism with green accent (`#22c55e`). Background: `#1a1f2e` (login) / `#0d1117` (app). Fonts: Nunito (display/brand), DM Sans (UI body). The palette is already established — maintain it across all new work. Anti-references: neon cyberpunk, consumer-app pastels, heavy glassmorphism.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over decoration** — every visual element earns its place by aiding comprehension or navigation.
2. **Consistent depth system** — neumorphism on auth pages, flat dark surfaces in the app interior. Don't mix the systems.
3. **Green means action** — `#22c55e` is reserved for primary CTAs and active/success states only. Don't use it decoratively.
4. **Motion serves state** — animate to communicate change (entrance, feedback, loading). Never animate for decoration.
5. **Restrained transitions** — ease-out-expo curves only. No bounce, no elastic, no spring overshoot.
